# Gold Karat (K) Guide

## Gold me commonly ye Karat (K) hote hain

| Karat | Purity | Gold % | Hallmark |
|---|---|---|---|
| 24K | Sabse pure | 99.9% | 999 |
| 23K | Bahut pure | 95.8% | 958 |
| 22K | Jewellery me common | 91.6% | 916 |
| 21K | Thoda kam pure | 87.5% | 875 |
| 18K | Diamond jewellery me common | 75% | 750 |
| 14K | Strong jewellery | 58.5% | 585 |
| 10K | Kam gold | 41.7% | 417 |

---

# India me sabse zyada kya chalta hai?

## 22K (916)

Use:
- Normal gold jewellery
- Chain
- Ring
- Mangalsutra
- Bangle

Sabse common India me.

---

## 18K (750)

Use:
- Diamond jewellery
- Stylish jewellery
- White gold
- Rose gold

Isme gold kam aur dusri metal zyada hoti hai, isliye strong hota hai.

---

## 24K (999)

Use:
- Coin
- Biscuit
- Investment

Isse normal jewellery kam banti hai kyuki bahut soft hota hai.

> # Market Dukandar Final price calculation

3.82 gram 18K gold ka ek realistic full calculation:

1) Gold Value

18K live rate ≈ ₹11,550/gm

3.82 × ₹11,550
= ₹44,121


2) Making Charge (15%)

₹44,121 × 15%
= ₹6,618


3) Wastage Charge (3%)

₹44,121 × 3%
= ₹1,323


4) Hallmark Charge

= ₹50

5) Subtotal

₹44,121 + ₹6,618 + ₹1,323 + ₹50
= ₹52,112


6) GST (3%)

₹52,112 × 3%
= ₹1,563


Final Approx Price

₹53,675

Ye normal showroom/jeweller ke hisab se realistic total hai.

Agar:

branded showroom hai → ₹55k–₹60k+

heavy design hai → making charge aur badhega

stone laga hai → aur increase hoga.
