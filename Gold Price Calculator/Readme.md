# Gold Karat (K) Guide

## Gold me commonly ye Karat (K) hote hain

| Karat | Purity | Gold % | Hallmark |
|---|---|---|---|
| 24K | Sabse pure | 99.9% | 999 |
| 23K | Bahut pure | 95.8% | 958 |
| 22K | Jewellery me common | 91.6% | 916 |
| 21K | Thoda kam pure | 87.5% | 875 |
| 18K | Diamond jewellery me common | 75% | 750 |
| 14K | Strong jewellery | 58.5% | 585 |
| 10K | Kam gold | 41.7% | 417 |

---

# India me sabse zyada kya chalta hai?

## 22K (916)

Use:
- Normal gold jewellery
- Chain
- Ring
- Mangalsutra
- Bangle

Sabse common India me.

---

## 18K (750)

Use:
- Diamond jewellery
- Stylish jewellery
- White gold
- Rose gold

Isme gold kam aur dusri metal zyada hoti hai, isliye strong hota hai.

---

## 24K (999)

Use:
- Coin
- Biscuit
- Investment

Isse normal jewellery kam banti hai kyuki bahut soft hota hai.

---

# Purity Formula

\[
Gold\ Purity\% = \frac{K}{24} \times 100
\]

## Example: 18K

\[
\frac{18}{24} \times 100 = 75\%
\]

Matlab:
18K me 75% asli gold hota hai.

---

# Example Calculation

Question:

\[
154160 \times \frac{18}{24}
\]

---

# Method 1 (Easy)

Pehle fraction simplify karo:

\[
\frac{18}{24} = \frac{3}{4}
\]

Ab question ban gaya:

\[
154160 \times \frac{3}{4}
\]

---

## Step 1: 154160 ko 4 se divide karo

\[
154160 \div 4 = 38540
\]

---

## Step 2: Jo answer aaya usko 3 se multiply karo

\[
38540 \times 3 = 115620
\]

---

# Final Answer

\[
154160 \times \frac{18}{24} = 115620
\]

Iska matlab:

Agar 24K gold ₹154160 hai,

to 18K gold ka approx rate ₹115620 hoga.
