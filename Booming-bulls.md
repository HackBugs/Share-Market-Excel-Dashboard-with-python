> # 3. The acronym **SRPSQT** in the context of a **Trading System** is likely a custom framework or personal mnemonic. Here's a commonly interpreted full form used by traders, especially in journaling or trade planning:

### ✅ **SRPSQT Full Form** (for trading):

1. **S** – Setup
2. **R** – Risk
3. **P** – Position size
4. **S** – Stop loss
5. **Q** – Quantity
6. **T** – Target

---

### 🧠 Explanation:

* **Setup** – What is the trade setup? (Pattern, technical signal, etc.)
* **Risk** – How much risk are you taking? (e.g., ₹500 per trade)
* **Position size** – Based on your risk, how many lots/shares?
* **Stop loss** – Where will you exit if it goes wrong?
* **Quantity** – Confirm the number of units/shares/lots.
* **Target** – What’s your profit goal?

---

### ⭐ **Favorites Indicators List (with Explanation in Hinglish)**

| 📌 **Indicator Name**                         | 📖 **Explanation (Hinglish)**                                                             |
| --------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Accumulation/Distribution**                 | Price aur volume ke basis pe batata hai ki market me paisa aa raha hai ya nikal raha hai. |
| **Average True Range (ATR)**                  | Market ki **volatility** (tezi ya mandi ka range) dikhata hai.                            |
| **Bollinger Bands**                           | Price upper ya lower band ko touch kare to overbought ya oversold condition dikhata hai.  |
| **Chaikin Money Flow (CMF)**                  | Volume aur price ke base pe batata hai ki buyers ya sellers ka pressure zyada hai.        |
| **Directional Movement Index (DMI)**          | Market me trend hai ya nahi, aur wo trend strong hai ya nahi, batata hai.                 |
| **Ichimoku Cloud**                            | Complex indicator hai jo **trend, support/resistance, aur momentum** sab dikhata hai.     |
| **MACD (Moving Avg. Convergence Divergence)** | Trend reversal aur momentum ke signals deta hai.                                          |
| **Moving Average (Simple)**                   | Price ka average nikalta hai, jisse trend samajhne me madad milti hai.                    |
| **Moving Average Exponential (EMA)**          | Recent price ko zyada weight deta hai, fast signals milte hain.                           |
| **On Balance Volume (OBV)**                   | Volume ka use karke trend confirm karta hai.                                              |
| **Pivot Points Standard**                     | Support aur resistance levels dikhata hai – trading decisions ke liye use hota hai.       |
| **PPO Divergence Alerts**                     | Price aur indicator me divergence ho to alert karta hai (similar to MACD).                |
| **Relative Strength Index (RSI)**             | 0–100 scale me batata hai ki stock **overbought (>70)** hai ya **oversold (<30)**.        |
| **Stochastic**                                | RSI jaisa hi hota hai, lekin fast signals deta hai for overbought/oversold.               |
