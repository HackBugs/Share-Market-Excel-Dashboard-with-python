## **20 Dark Psychology Manipulation Tactics**

1. **Gaslighting** – Kisi ko apne perception pe doubt karwana (*"tumhe galat yaad hai"*)
2. **Love Bombing** – Starting me extreme affection, baad me control lena.
3. **Triangulation** – Teesre insaan ko laa kar jealousy create karna.
4. **Silent Treatment** – Ignore karke punishment dena.
5. **Guilt Tripping** – Tumhe guilty feel kara ke apna kaam nikalna.
6. **Neuro-Linguistic Anchoring** – Certain words/tones se tumhare emotions trigger karna.
7. **Mirroring** – Tumhare behavior copy karke tumhara trust jeetna.
8. **Future Faking** – Bade-bade future promises karke present me benefit lena.
9. **Intermittent Reinforcement** – Kabhi reward, kabhi punishment, taaki tum addicted raho.
10. **Projection** – Apni galti tum par dal dena.
11. **Victim Playing** – Hamesha apne aap ko bechara dikhana sympathy ke liye.
12. **Hoovering** – Breakup ke baad phir se kheench ke apne control me lana.
13. **Negging** – Hidden insults dekar tumhara self-esteem todna.
14. **Information Control** – Tumse kuch sach chhupana ya galat batana.
15. **Gaslight by Consensus** – Multiple log mil ke tumhe galat prove karna.
16. **Time Pressure** – Jaldi decision lene ka pressure daalna.
17. **Overloading** – Itni info dena ki tum confuse ho jao.
18. **Foot-in-the-door** – Pehle chhoti request, phir badi request rakhna.
19. **Door-in-the-face** – Pehle bada demand rakhna (jo tum refuse karo) phir chhoti demand rakhna jo tum accept kar lo.
20. **Fear Appeal** – Dar dikhakar decision manipulate karna.

> **20 dark psychology manipulation tactics**

---

### **1. Gaslighting**

Koi tumhe baar-baar bole ki “tum galat ho” ya “tumhe yaad nahi hai sahi,” jabki tum sahi ho.
🎯 Purpose – Tumhare dimaag me doubt create karna.

---

### **2. Love Bombing**

Shuru me itna pyaar, compliments, gifts, attention dena ki tum unpe depend ho jao.
🎯 Baad me wo control le lete hain.

---

### **3. Triangulation**

Teesra insaan laa ke jealousy ya insecurity create karna — jaise ex ka naam lena.
🎯 Tum hamesha insecure raho.

---

### **4. Silent Treatment**

Bina reason tumse baat band kar dena.
🎯 Tum tension me aa jao aur sorry bol do.

---

### **5. Guilt Tripping**

Tumse kahe “Tumne mere liye kabhi kuch nahi kiya” jab tumne kaafi kiya ho.
🎯 Tum guilty feel karo aur wo apna kaam nikal le.

---

### **6. Neuro-Linguistic Anchoring**

Certain words, smell, ya sound ko tumhare emotions se link kar dena.
🎯 Baad me wo trigger karke tumhara reaction control karte hain.

---

### **7. Mirroring**

Tumhari body language, words copy karke tumhara trust jeetna.
🎯 Tum socho “ye to mera hi type ka hai.”

---

### **8. Future Faking**

Bade-bade promises — “Hum shaadi karenge”, “business shuru karenge” — but sirf tumse abhi kaam nikalne ke liye.

---

### **9. Intermittent Reinforcement**

Kabhi reward, kabhi ignore — tum emotionally hooked raho.
🎯 Tum socho “shayad agla time wo mujhe appreciate kare.”

---

### **10. Projection**

Apni galti tum par daal dena — jaise wo cheating kare aur bole “Tum flirt karte ho.”

---

### **11. Victim Playing**

Har baat me apne aap ko bechara dikhana taaki sympathy mile.

---

### **12. Hoovering**

Breakup ke baad suddenly pyaar dikhana taaki tum wapas aa jao.

---

### **13. Negging**

Thoda insult + thoda compliment — “Tum smart ho, bas thoda aur fit ho jao.”
🎯 Self-esteem kam karna.

---

### **14. Information Control**

Sach chhupana ya galat info dena taaki tum reality na samjho.

---

### **15. Gaslight by Consensus**

Multiple log milke tumhe convince karna ki tum galat ho.

---

### **16. Time Pressure**

Decision lene ka jaldi pressure — “Abhi decide karo, offer khatam ho jayega.”

---

### **17. Overloading**

Itni information dena ki tum confuse ho jao aur bas unki baat maan lo.

---

### **18. Foot-in-the-door**

Chhoti request pe haan bulwana, fir gradually badi request karna.

---

### **19. Door-in-the-face**

Pehle ek bada demand jo tum refuse karo, fir chhota demand jo tum accept kar lo.

---

### **20. Fear Appeal**

Dar dikhakar control lena — “Agar tumne meri baat nahi maani to problem ho jayegi.”
