> ## Investopedia ke mutabik, intraday ya day trading mein kuch popular strategies hain jo traders short-term price movements ka fayda uthane ke liye istemal karte hain. Yahan kuch mukhya strategies ko simple Hinglish mein samjhaya gaya hai:([Wikipedia][1])

---

### 1. **Scalping (बहुत छोटे मुनाफे के लिए तेज़ ट्रेडिंग)**

Scalping ek strategy hai jisme trader bahut chhoti price movements ka fayda uthata hai. Isme positions kuch seconds ya minutes ke liye hi rakhi jati hain. Scalpers din bhar mein kai trades karte hain, har ek se chhota moolya labh kamane ke liye. Yeh strategy high liquidity aur volatility wale stocks ke liye upyogi hoti hai. ([Investopedia][2])

---

### 2. **Range Trading (Support aur Resistance ke beech trading)**

Is strategy mein traders un stocks ko target karte hain jo ek certain price range mein trade kar rahe hote hain. Jab price support level ke paas hota hai, tab buy karte hain, aur jab resistance level ke paas hota hai, tab sell karte hain. Yeh strategy tab kaam karti hai jab market mein koi clear trend nahi hota.&#x20;

---

### 3. **News-Based Trading (Samachar ke aadhar par trading)**

Isme traders market news ya events jaise earnings reports, economic indicators, ya political events ke aadhar par trades karte hain. News ke turant baad market mein volatility badh jati hai, jiska fayda uthaya jata hai. Yeh strategy high risk aur high reward dono ho sakti hai.&#x20;

---

### 4. **Trend Following (Trend ke saath trading)**

Is strategy mein traders existing market trend ka anusaran karte hain. Agar price upar ja raha hai to buy karte hain, aur agar neeche ja raha hai to sell karte hain. Moving averages jaise technical indicators ka upyog karke trend identify kiya jata hai. ([Investopedia][3], [Investopedia][4])

---

### 5. **Momentum Trading (Tezi ya Mandi ke momentum ka fayda uthana)**

Isme traders un stocks ko target karte hain jo strong price movement dikha rahe hote hain, high volume ke saath. Yeh strategy short-term price spikes ka fayda uthati hai. Traders technical indicators jaise RSI ya MACD ka upyog karke entry aur exit points decide karte hain.

---

### **Intraday Trading ke Liye Kuch Zaroori Tips:**

* **High Liquidity Stocks:** Un stocks mein trade karein jisme high trading volume ho, taki aasani se entry aur exit ho sake.
* **Stop-Loss ka Upyog:** Har trade ke liye stop-loss set karein, taki bade nuksan se bacha ja sake.
* **Technical Analysis Seekhein:** Charts aur indicators ka upyog karke market trends aur patterns samjhein.
* **Discipline aur Patience:** Emotional decisions se bachein aur apne trading plan par stick karein.([Wikipedia][5])
