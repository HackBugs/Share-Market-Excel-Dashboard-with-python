1. **Ramayan aur Mahabharat ke scenes** ko unhone itne sundar aur real tarike se dikhaya ki logon ka **devi-devtaon ka kalpnik roop** ban gaya.

   * Jaise: Lakshmi ji, Saraswati ji, Sita ji, Damayanti, Shakuntala, etc.

2. **Printing press** se unhone apne paintings ko aam janta tak pahunchaya.
   ➤ **Pehli baar logon ne bhagwan ke chitr gharon mein dekhe!**

3. **Indian calendar art** ki shuruaat bhi indirectly unhi ki wajah se hui.
